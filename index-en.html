About me

I'm a programmer with experience developing videogames in Unity using C#. I'm a creative, motivated and committed person, I can learn quickly and I'm willing to learn a little more every day to continue growing personally and professionally. I have studied Unity through courses and applied my knowledge in different personal and professional projects. I have worked professionally in projects with ToroLabs Colombia team, developing educational simulators for virtual learning.


Also, I'm currently finishing my degree in Computer Science, which has allowed me to grow as a programmer capable of solving problems applying good practices and developing clean, readable and easy maintenance code. In my career, I learned to develop software with several programming languages for different areas, however, I specialized in video game development since I'm passionate about creating interactive and immersive experiences that combine art and programming.
